welcome to Greek Delights, a restaurant in Berlin, Germany, offering the best of Greek cuisine. Enjoy our delicious Greek dishes in a cozy and friendly atmosphere.

Traditionally built, warm welcoming venue where you can enjoy a fine Restaurant & Bar experience. During the month and at special times we also have entertainment evenings from relaxing piano playing to live bands.

Facilities

In April 2015 we re-opened with an extension almost doubling the original size, using local contemporary stone giving a country air welcoming feel from the outside. Whilst inside using light oak suspended beams and a feature fireplace with wood burning stove for those cooler days. Our alfresco eatery offers an oak wood oven baked pizzeria, which are available throughout the day & evenings. All food is freshly prepared on site using produce, wherever possible, sourced locally within a 5 mile radius.

Accessibility

Disabled parking access and toilet facilities throughout. We will also try to accommodate any and all food allergies.
